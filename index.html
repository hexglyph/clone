Roadmap

Rename Event:

Personalize your Mecha by adding an "on-chain" name or "alias" to your Mecha.

Every Mecha has unique personality and aesthetic traits and deserves a nomenclature. We think calling NFTs by a name - builts this unique connection between collectors and their NFTs. This alias also adds a further layer of interactivity in the Mechaverse.

Live on our portal immediately after the public reveal.

Egg Hunt:

Find eggs & keys hidden in artwork and properties. Use them to solve riddles and win airdrops, NFTs & a grand prize. Exclusive for Mecha NFT holders.

Eggs and hidden references embedded in the artwork and metadata, find eggs and keys, use them to solve 12 riddles and gain access to an ETH wallet. Eggs bring an inbuilt gamification and interactivity with our artwork and attract new users to participate & be a part of Mechaverse.

Eggs are already part of the artwork, Egg-hunt event will start soon after the public reveal.

Phase 2

Token & P2E

Q3-Q4' 2022

Token Distribution/ NFT Staking::

Token generation, distribution and airdrops of $MECHA tokens, We may decide to distribute using NFT staking or simple drops for Mecha NFT owners.

More details on distribution and tokenomics will be announced soon.

An immersive & interactive P2E game:

An interactive, strategy P2E game, where choices made by participants decide the outcome and winning "Faction". Rewards will be distributed to the winning "Faction". A P2E game where you don't need to spend hours playing the same environment but rather all participants build a script of an episode in Mechaverse. Think Bandersnatch + Hunger Games.

All choices and narratives will be provided and recorded by a story generator AI, continuously improving to generate better episodes.

Breeding:

Breed your Mechas to create a unique Mecha from a different generation (both Minus and Plus Gens).

We plan to show the evolution of Mechas using Minus and Plus Gens. Depending upon the "Game Traits" of parent Mechas - a new Mecha will be bred.

All generations will have different design traits, new factions & game traits.
